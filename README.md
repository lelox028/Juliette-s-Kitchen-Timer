# 🍰 Juliette's Kitchen Timer

*A cozy pixel-art Pomodoro timer where a tiny baker cat helps you stay focused.*

**Juliette's Kitchen Timer** is a lightweight Progressive Web App designed to help you focus using the Pomodoro technique — with the company of **Juliette**, a tiny pastry-chef cat who bakes delicious treats while you work.

When you're focusing, Juliette is busy baking cakes.
When it's time to rest, she curls up and naps beside the oven. 🐱💤

Simple, cozy, and distraction-free.

## 🧠 What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro technique is a time management method based on short, focused work sessions separated by breaks.

Typical cycle:

1. **Focus session** — 25 minutes
2. **Short break** — 5 minutes
3. Repeat 4 times
4. **Long break** — 15–20 minutes

Juliette's Kitchen Timer manages the cycle automatically so you can stay focused while Juliette keeps the kitchen running.

## 🐱 Meet Juliette

Juliette is a tiny baker cat who keeps you company while you work.

States:

* 🍰 **Baking** — during focus sessions
* 💤 **Sleeping** — during breaks
* 🎉 **Celebrating** — when a session finishes

All animations are handmade pixel art.
